Ayurveda, a traditional Indian system of medicine, holds immense importance in the country's healthcare heritage. Rooted in ancient texts, Ayurveda emphasizes holistic well-being by balancing one's mind, body, and spirit. It offers personalized treatment plans tailored to individual constitution (Prakriti). While both Ayurveda and allopathy have their merits, Ayurveda's natural approach is often preferred for chronic conditions and maintaining overall health, as it seeks to address the root causes rather than merely alleviating symptoms, making it a valuable complement to modern medicine.
